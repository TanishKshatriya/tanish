Who We Are

A Two-Person
Studio of Darkness

Darkphobia Games is a two-member independent studio dedicated to crafting atmospheric horror experiences that linger long after the screen goes dark.

We believe fear is an art form. Each of our games is built around a single haunting premise — a lighthouse, a graveyard, an ancient tomb — and we sculpt every detail to pull you deeper into the dread.
